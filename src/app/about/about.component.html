We are students of the Aristotle University of Thessaloniki

We are simply a group of people with a plan to make our University , and why not our city , country or even the world , a better and healthier place for their people. Our goal for the time being is to inspire other students to cooperate with us into taking better care of our University by organizing cleaning events, growing plants, and other respectful events towards the environment of the place we spend a lot of our time at!
